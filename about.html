About Wisp

Wisp is a lightweight web browser based on the NetSurf browser engine and heavily inspired by its philosophy of speed and efficiency. It also incorporates valuable contributions and code from the NeoSurf project.

Acknowledgments

We are highly grateful to the developers of both NetSurf and NeoSurf. Without their hard work, dedication, and open-source contributions, this project would not have been possible.

Development Process

Wisp utilizes AI technology to enhance and modernize the original codebase. However, all AI-generated contributions are strictly monitored, guided, and reviewed by an experienced human developer to ensure quality, security, and performance.
